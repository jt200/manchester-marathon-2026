# Manchester Marathon 2026 - Official Route

**Date:** Sunday, 19 April 2026  
**Start:** 8:55 AM from Old Trafford car park (Bridgewater Way)  
**Finish:** Oxford Road, outside University of Manchester  
**Distance:** 42.2 km (26.2 miles)  

## Route Segments

### Start Zone (km 0-2)
- **Location:** Old Trafford car park, Bridgewater Way
- **Landmarks:** Manchester United ground, White City retail park
- **Key feature:** All runners depart here

### South Loop (km 2-20)
- **Chester Road** → Castlefield → Deansgate → Stretford
- **Stretford area** (km ~5-8): South of city centre
- **Sale** (km ~11-13): Waterside area
- **Timperley** (km ~13-15): Small town south of Sale
- **Altrincham** (km ~15-18): Southernmost point, turnaround section
- **Baguley** (km ~18-20): South-east turnaround toward home

### Return Loop (km 20-42.2)
- **Chorlton** (km ~22-25): Major support point on return
- **Whalley Range** (km ~25-28): Heading north back to city
- **Hulme** (km ~28-32): Approaching city centre
- **City Centre Sprint** (km ~35-42): Final approach
- **Oxford Road finish** (km 42.2): Outside Whitworth Hall

## Key Facts
- **Runner speed (your friend):** 11.10 km/h
- **Your jogging speed:** 7-10 min/km (6-8.5 km/h)
- **Estimated finish time:** ~3 hours 48 minutes (12:43 PM)
- **Flat course** with good support throughout
