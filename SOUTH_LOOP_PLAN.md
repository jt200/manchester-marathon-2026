- Why: Spacious, outdoor areas

4. Stay until 11:45 AM

- Jog toward Sale town centre

## Contingencies

**What if you're running late?**
- Jog faster between pubs (even 7 min/km is doable)
- Skip snacks until pub stops
- Use location sharing with friend to know exactly where she is

**What if pubs are packed?**
- Have backup pubs nearby (other options listed above)
- Waterside locations often have outdoor space
- Arrive 5-10 mins early if possible

**What if she's running ahead/behind schedule?**
- She's targeting 4 hours = 10.55 km/h
- If she's faster: she'll pass pubs earlier than estimated
- If she's slower: she'll pass later
- The 30-min pub windows are generous enough to catch her either way

**What if you miss her at a pub?**
- You'll likely see her at the next one
- She's not lapping—just passing through once
- Get a photo/video for proof 😄
