# Pub Crawl Routes (Redesigned)

## The Dynamic

**She's 40% faster than your jogging pace.** So the strategy flips:

Instead of: "Jog between pubs and catch her"  
**Do this:** "Jog ahead, position at a pub, she catches up and passes you"

All three options let you see her pass while you're drinking. The difference is how many times and which parts of the route.

---

## Option 2: South Outbound + Return Leg
**Pubs:** 3 | **Total running:** ~11km | **Sees her:** 3 times  
**Best for:** See her on the outbound AND the return leg

### Route Map
```
Stretford (km 5, outbound)
    ↓ jog 5km south (40 mins)
Sale (km ~11, outbound)
    ↓ jog 5km north/northwest cutting corners (40 mins)
Chorlton (km 25, return leg)
    ↓ jog 3km to finish area (24 mins)
City Centre (finish)
```

### Timeline

**Pub #1: Stretford** (11:15-11:45 AM)
- She passes at **11:38 AM** ✓
- Leave at 11:45 (friend at km 6.2)

**Transit: Stretford → Sale**
- Jog 5km south, 40 mins
- Arrive 12:25 PM (friend at km 10.4)

**Pub #2: Sale Waterside** (12:25-12:55 PM)
- She passes at **~12:30 PM** (partway through) ✓
- Leave at 12:55 (friend at km 15.8)

**Transit: Sale → Chorlton (cutting across)**
- Jog 5km northwest (cuts the long way around the marathon route), 40 mins
- Arrive 1:35 PM
- Friend is at km ~25 at 1:31 PM
- **Timing is tight—you might catch her as she's arriving**

**Pub #3: Chorlton High Road** (1:35-2:05 PM)
- She passes at **~1:31-1:45 PM** (right at arrival) ⚠️
- You might see her but timing is snug
- If timing misses: shift to watching her at the finish instead

**Optional: City Centre Finish Area**
- Jog 3km into city centre, 24 mins
- Arrive 2:29 PM
- Be there to watch her finish at 3:10 PM

### Total Running: 10-11km (includes jog to finish)

### Why This Works
- ✅ See her outbound twice (Stretford, Sale)
- ✅ See her on return or at finish
- ✅ More variety of scenery
- ⚠️ Chorlton timing is tight; be ready with the camera

## Recommendation: **Go with Option 1 or 2**

Both work with ~10km running and 3 pubs. Pick based on:

- **Option 1 (South Loop):** Easiest logistics, guaranteed timing, all south Manchester
- **Option 2 (Out + Return):** More scenic, see her on different legs, finish-line energy

---

## Key Principles for All Routes

1. **Jog AHEAD, not behind**
   - Position pubs ahead of where she currently is
   - She catches you while you're having a pint

2. **Use pub time as buffer**
   - Arrive 5-10 mins before she does (if you can)
   - Have your pint while she passes
   - She's gone, you've got a story

3. **Route cuts matter**
   - Don't follow the full marathon loop between pubs
   - Cut northwest/across neighbourhoods where possible
   - Saves km and time

4. **Timing is tight but doable**
   - Friend pace: 10.55 km/h
   - Your jog pace: 8 min/km (7.5 km/h average)
   - You can position 5-10 mins ahead, reliably

5. **Have a phone tracker**
   - Ask her to share location during race
   - Lets you adjust if you're running late

---

## Pub Selection Checklist

For each location, pick pubs that:
- ✅ Are directly on the marathon route (so she runs past)
- ✅ Have outdoor seating/windows (to see her coming)
- ✅ Get decent crowds on race day (festive vibes)
- ✅ Serve food (you'll be hungry)
- ❌ Avoid: interior-only, off the route, quiet pubs
