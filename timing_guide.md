## Pro Tips
1. **Stay ON the route:** Position pubs directly on marathon streets so you can literally watch her run past
2. **Ask bar staff:** They'll know if the route runs directly past—some pubs have prime viewing
3. **Arrive early:** Popular spectator spots fill up; be there 15 mins before she's due
4. **Phone tracking:** If she has her phone on, share location during the race to track her live
5. **Cheering angle:** Get on the pavement near the pub so you can cheer her on without leaving your drink!
